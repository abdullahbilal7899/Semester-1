OUR TEAM


Owner/Founder

Gwendolyn Rogers

Some people express themselves in words; authors pen the lives of entire heroes through words alone. Others express themselves through sound; composers script a beautiful story entirely through sound. But how do you take a wonderful piece of art, and truly experience it? True, you can see it and hear it, but how can you savor it? Confectioner Gwendolyn Rogers tells her story through the sumptuous experience of taste. As the orders started getting larger, events were being booked more frequently, and her kitchen seemed ever smaller, Gwendolyn realized her vision: the Yame Bakery Shop. Collecting her thousands of recipes, Gwendolyn set up shop in a picturesque Broad Ripple cottage that she remodeled and turned into the now Yame Bakery Shop. It houses a gourmet kitchen with a French flair, world-class chefs, and one of a kind exquisite desserts, Cakes for any Occasion, Cupcakes & Cookies, Salty Pastry and more. Gwendolyn has been featured on ‘Home & Family’ on the Hallmark Channel and Food Network’s ‘Guilty Pleasures’.
