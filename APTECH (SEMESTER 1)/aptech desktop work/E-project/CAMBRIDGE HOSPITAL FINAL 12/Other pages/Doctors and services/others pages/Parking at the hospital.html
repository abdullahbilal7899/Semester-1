Parking at the hospital



PARKING




Available Parking


Patients and visitors can park in Visitor
Parking Lot #3 (to the side and rear of the
hospital) and Visitor Lot #4 (front). To
access both parking lots, please use the
entrance by Wing C (Ambulatory Care) at
the new traffic lights.




Visitor Lot Information

Lot #3 & Lot #4

These parking lots are gated and can only be accessed from the entrance by Wing C. You will need to press a button to get a parking stub and lift the gate. This stub will be used to pay for the time spent in the parking lot.

For your convenience, you may also use your credit card to enter both parking lots. If you do, you will not get a parking stub. When you leave, simply use the same card to open the gate.





* Packs are 50% off the regular rate. Passes are transferable and valid for one (1) year from date of purchase. Packs can only be purchased at the Parking Office 0900 - 1400, Monday to Friday or at Security after hours, weekends and holidays. For more information, contact the Parking Office at 519.621.2333 ext. 1359.
