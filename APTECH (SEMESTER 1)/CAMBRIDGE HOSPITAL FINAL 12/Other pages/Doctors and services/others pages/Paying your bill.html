HOW TO PAY YOUR BILL




Payment Options

OHIP will cover many of the health services you receive in hospital, such as the cost of a standard ward room. There are some supplies and services that are not covered by OHIP and you may receive a bill for them. For example, if you request a semi-private or rent a television while in hospital, this will incur a cost to you. For your convenience, you may pay your bill using one of the following methods. When you are prepared to settle your bill, please have your patient account number with you.

By Mail addressed to:

Attn. Patient Accounts
