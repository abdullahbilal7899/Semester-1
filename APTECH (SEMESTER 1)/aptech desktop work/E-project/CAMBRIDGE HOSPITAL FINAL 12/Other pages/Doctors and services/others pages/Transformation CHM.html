TRANSFORMATION CHM



Welcome to “Transformation CMH,” where you
will find information and updates about our new hospital build.

YES! A brand new hospital for the residents of Cambridge and North Dumfries
is well udner way. The construction comprises a new patient care tower and
complete renovation of the existing patient care wing, which will provide the
necessary space for CMH to bring new and expanded clinical services
to our community.


In total, the project includes 240,000 square feet of new construction and 150, 000
square feet of renovations to the current Wing B. The total cost of the project is
valued at about $187 million.


A community component for funding the Cambridge Memorial Hospital Capital
Redevelopment Project has been raised by the Region of Waterloo, the City of
Cambridge and the community through the One Voice, One Vision campaign.
Additional funds to purchase the crucial life-saving technology and equipment that is
needed to fill this essential expansion are needed.
Please contact the CMH Foundation for more information.
