Patient Rooms




We offer our guests a variety of arrangements for their stay with us from ultra luxury suits to simpler ward structured beds. One thing we promise that no matter whether you are a luxury dweller or in our general ward, you shall receive the same level of expert medical care by our consultants and other staff as that is our main priority above all else. If your condition requires most protected and monitored care, you may be placed in our state of the art intensive care beds.
Each of our rooms are equipped with various items to make you feel at home including LED LCD video and television systems, a in-room fridge to store your drinks and other perishables, and optional pullout couch in case there is a need for an attendant to stay with you and monitor your condition.
Your rooms / bathroom are antiseptically cleaned on a daily basis to ensure the highest of sanitary standards. If you require an extra cleaning at any time, please contact your on hand nurse for assistance.
