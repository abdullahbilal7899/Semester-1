Cambridge Specialty Hospitals,
OMR is a new milestone in our journey of healing that has touched over 40 million lives, across 120 countries. The multi-Specialty tertiary care hospital promises to take world-class health-care to Chennai’s IT corridor, and the areas neighbouring OMR.
Designed to be Chennai’s first digital hospital, it will be characterized by customized workflows that leverage cutting edge technology, and harness the immense power of Health IT. Electronic medical records that can be accessed from anywhere, convenient e-payment systems, and an automated medication safety shield are just some of the unique features of the digitally empowered Apollo Specialty Hospitals, OMR.
The facility offers high-end tertiary care across various medical disciplines with special focus on Neurology, Trauma, Cardio Thoracic and Cardiology. As an integral part of the group, the 150 bedded hospital is a perfect balance of excellent infrastructure, latest technology and dedicated manpower, all orchestrated to provide patient services of the highest standards. The magic of technology is evident in many thoughtful facets of the new hospital. The innovative ‘I SEE U’ is a virtual visitor solution that uses new age imaging to allow virtual patient visits. Health checks will be powered by clinically profound prescriptive analytics, enabling personalized healthcare to meet the specific individual’s needs.
The Apollo Hospitals has established Centres of Excellence across several key specialties. The goal of each Centre of Excellence is to ensure that care of the highest possible standards is offered whilst forging ahead in research, meeting and surpassing benchmarks set by the world’s best healthcare providers, tracking and standardizing best practices and also by achieving excellence in academics.
