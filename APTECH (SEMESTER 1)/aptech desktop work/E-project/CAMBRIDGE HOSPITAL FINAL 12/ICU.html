I C U

I C U


The ICU is located on the 1st floor and has 22 beds. It is a multi-functional ICU for managing critically ill patients. All the Units are functionally independent with dedicated Nursing station, central monitoring, clean & dirty utility areas and are manned by dedicated specialty nurses. The ICUs are equipped with state of the art monitoring system and ventilators. Regular monitoring includes ECG, arrhythmia and ST-T segment evaluation, central and core temperature, NIBP, ABP, CVP, PAP, ETCO2, ICP and intraabdominal pressures. The facilities of Echocardiography, ultrasound, colour Doppler and x-ray are available round the clock. The ICU is manned 24 x 7 by experienced medical intensivists. We cater to sick medical patients like poisoning & envenomation, polytrauma and head injury, septic shock, acute respiratory distress syndrome, acute pancreatitis, complicated pneumonia, asthma & COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease).
The various infrastructure facilities available in the Cambridge Care at Hospital include:

  • ECG Monitor
  • Cardiac Monitoring Stations
  • Pulse Oximeters
  • Infusion pumps
  • Infusion syringe pumps
  • Defibrillator
  • Ventilators
  • Invasive BP Monitoring
  • In-house ABG analysis
  • Nebulizers
